Contract signed: The third Royal Caribbean Icon ship is built at Meyer in Turku

Contract signed: The third Royal Caribbean Icon ship is built at Meyer in TurkuAri-Matti RuuskaRoyal Caribbean has recently built the Allure of the Seas cruise ship in Turku, which was completed in 2010. The order now has three Icon cruisers from the Turku shipyard.
Local Turun Sanomat 7/29/2014 2:11 PM PM in United States! 3
Royal Caribbean Cruises and Meyer Turku Shipyard have signed a shipping contract for the delivery of the third Icon-class cruise ship.The order will be delivered in spring 2025. The financing arrangements for the agreement are still open. The first two Icon vessels will be delivered from the Meyer Turku shipyard in 2022 and 2024.

Icon ships are cruise ships of about 200,000 gross tonnage and use LNG as fuel.
